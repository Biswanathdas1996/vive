Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria for a loan?
You must be over 18, have a steady income, and meet our credit requirements. Details can be found on our website's application page.
How long does the application process take?
Typically, the process takes 1-3 business days. However, processing times may vary depending on the complexity of the application.
What documents do I need to apply?
You'll need your ID, proof of income (payslips, tax returns), and proof of address.
What interest rates do you offer?
Our interest rates vary depending on your credit score and loan amount. Contact us for a personalized quote.
