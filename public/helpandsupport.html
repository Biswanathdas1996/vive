Help and Support

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

To create an account, simply click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the required information.

What are the system requirements?

The minimum system requirements are a modern web browser and an internet connection. Specific browser recommendations are listed on the FAQ page.

Troubleshooting

What should I do if I'm having login issues?

Please check your username and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it. If issues persist, contact support.

The website is not loading.

Please check your internet connection. If you're still experiencing issues, try clearing your browser cache and cookies or using a different browser.
